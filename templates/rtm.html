About This Suite

Our Ocean View Suite offers an unparalleled experience of luxury and comfort. Spanning 850 square feet, this elegant suite features floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the stunning ocean panorama. The suite features a king-sized canopy bed with premium linens, a spacious living area with a plush sectional sofa, and a private balcony with comfortable seating. The marble bathroom includes a deep soaking tub, walk-in rain shower, and exclusive luxury amenities.
